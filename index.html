Common questions

My punches aren't counting.
Bag Mode needs the physical Rage-Bag, plus camera and mic access. Prop the phone so it can see your upper body, clear your space, and make sure the room isn't too dark. Soft taps may not register — throw real punches.
Does FUNPUNCH record me?
No. The camera and mic run entirely on your phone. Nothing is recorded, saved, or uploaded. We don't collect anything ourselves. See the privacy policy below for the full breakdown.
Do I need an account?
No account, no sign-up. Your name, ranks, and personal bests live on your device.
The camera or mic won't turn on.
Open the iPhone Settings app, scroll to FUNPUNCH, and switch Camera and Microphone on. Then reopen the app.
How do I reset my stats?
Open Settings inside the app. You'll find the option to clear your progress there. This can't be undone.
Where do I get a Rage-Bag?
Bag Mode pairs with the physical Rage-Bag. Email us using the button above and we'll point you to it.
